## 🎯 Purpose

This hub aggregates the latest clinical evidence from ClinicalTrials.gov on ACL rehabilitation for female athletes. It is designed to:

- Give sports medicine clinicians a **quick-reference** for active and completed trials
- Document **rehabilitation protocols** being studied across institutions
- Surface **research trends** by trial phase and recruitment status
- Serve as a **living document** — update as new trials emerge

## 📊 Research Landscape (338 Total ACL Rehab Trials on ClinicalTrials.gov)

- **36%** of trials are completed
- **22%** are actively recruiting
- **76%** are non-phase (observational / behavioral / device studies)
- Only **~7%** are formal Phase 2–4 drug or device trials
